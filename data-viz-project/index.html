UMD enrolled students test score behavior changes admist strict admissions

By Nicole Pilsbury

The University of Maryland is increasing in prestige after being named a "Public Ivy" by Forbes magazine, therefore increasing the university's application numbers. Despite the increased number of applications, UMD's enrollment number stays consistent around 6,000 per year- meaning a smaller percent of students who apply are admitted. With the competition between prospective students rising, data has shown that the number of students who have waived their test scores has increased from 2022.
